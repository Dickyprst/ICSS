About to ICSS 2022

International Conference on Social Sciences (ICSS) is a conference for those who are interested to present papers in all fields of social sciences, humanities, and education. Conference topics focus on several disciplines including Law, Education, Cultural and Communication Studies, History, Economic, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, Art and Humanities, Language and Library Sciences, Human Literacy, and Administration Sciences. This conference is a forum for discussion between various parties such as academics, policy makers and social practitioners. It is also expected that the conference can show various kinds of cultural differences throughout the world. The additional purpose of this conference is to provide a place for academics and professionals with interdisciplinary / multi-disciplinary interests related to social sciences to meet and interact with members both within and outside their own disciplines. Selected papers that have been reviewed will be published in the Proceedings Indexed by Scopus / Web of Science /.
